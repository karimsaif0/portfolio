Nova Banking App

Redesigning the mobile banking experience for 2M+ users, making financial management intuitive and accessible.

Overview

Nova Bank approached us with a challenge: their mobile app, while feature-rich, was seeing declining user engagement and rising support tickets. Users found it difficult to complete basic tasks, and the visual design felt outdated compared to competitors.

Our goal was to completely reimagine the banking experience—making it not just functional, but delightful. We wanted users to feel confident and in control of their finances, not overwhelmed or anxious.

The Challenge

The Problem

User Drop-off: 68% of users abandoned the app within the first 3 sessions.

Support Load: 40% of support tickets were about basic tasks users couldn't find.

NPS Score: Standing at 23, significantly below industry average of 45.

Our Approach

Research-First: Deep dive into user behaviors, pain points, and mental models.

Simplify: Reduce cognitive load by 60% through smart information architecture.

Delight: Add micro-interactions that make banking feel less like a chore.

Design Process

01

Research & Discovery

We conducted extensive user research to understand the real needs and frustrations of Nova's customers.

  • 30 user interviews across different demographics
  • Analysis of 10,000+ support tickets
  • Competitive audit of 15 banking apps
  • Stakeholder interviews with bank executives
  • User journey mapping workshops
02

Defining the Problem

Synthesizing research findings to identify key opportunities and define actionable problem statements.

  • Created user personas: Sarah (Busy Professional), Mike (Family Man), Lisa (Student)
  • Identified 5 critical user flows for redesign
  • Prioritized features based on user impact vs. effort
  • Established design principles and success metrics
03

Ideation & Prototyping

Generating and testing ideas through rapid prototyping and iterative design.

  • Created 50+ wireframe concepts
  • Conducted 3 rounds of design studio sessions
  • Built interactive prototypes in Figma
  • Performed 5 usability tests with early prototypes
04

Design & Refinement

Polishing the design system and creating high-fidelity interfaces with attention to every detail.

  • Developed comprehensive design system with 120+ components
  • Created motion guidelines for micro-interactions
  • Designed accessibility-first components (WCAG 2.1 AA)
  • Produced developer handoff documentation

Design Solutions

The redesigned app features a clean, modern interface with a focus on clarity and ease of use. Every element was carefully considered to reduce cognitive load and guide users naturally through tasks.

Results & Impact

The redesigned app launched in June 2024 to existing users, with impressive results across all key metrics. The new design has transformed how users interact with their finances.

45%
Increase in User Engagement
-60%
Reduction in Support Tickets

Net Promoter Score (up from 23)

Key Takeaways

Research is Everything

The depth of our research directly correlated with the success of the final product. Understanding users' mental models was crucial to creating intuitive flows.

Less is More

Simplifying the interface by removing rarely-used features and consolidating similar actions dramatically improved task completion rates.

Cross-Functional Collaboration

Close partnership with engineering from day one ensured our designs were not only beautiful but technically feasible and performant.
